About BugYou

BugYou is a platform designed for the modern programmer — where debugging isn't just a skill, it's a superpower.

In a world rapidly evolving with AI-generated code, the need to write from scratch is decreasing. But the demand for problem solvers — people who can fix, optimize, and understand what's under the hood — is skyrocketing.

That's where BugYou comes in. We're not your typical coding platform. We focus on the art of debugging — turning you into a code detective who can spot, analyze, and fix bugs with ninja-like precision.
