About BugYou

BugYou is a platform designed for the modern programmer — where debugging isn't just a skill, it's a superpower.

In a world rapidly evolving with AI-generated code, the need to write from scratch is decreasing. But the demand for problem solvers — people who can fix, optimize, and understand what's under the hood — is skyrocketing.

That's where BugYou comes in. It's not your typical coding site. We focus on:

Debugging challenges styled like LeetCode problems — from logical bugs to runtime surprises.
Error analysis tasks that sharpen your eye for detail and edge cases.
Logic building exercises that help you approach code like a detective, not just a coder.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, BugYou will help you:

Build core logic skills.
Recognize hidden errors in complex code.
Become more AI-proof by doing what AI can't: think critically.

We're here to make debugging fun, competitive, and addictive. Ready to train your bug radar?
